About

Hi, I'm Angelica!

I'm currently a Product Manager for Mitek Systems, building digital identity verification products for highly regulated markets. Before that, I worked as a Program Manager for LabKey Software, providing open source data management software for scientific researchers.

I love the dynamic nature of both product management and technology. Naturally, I'm drawn to finding answers to the question "why", analyzing trade-offs, and rallying people to a shared vision. I'm most passionate about products focused on compliance, trust and security, and I thrive in environments that value being a good human being and doing good for the rest of the world.

What did you do before PM?

Between fun/school/work, I've dabbled in fields including: web development, database management, SEO/SEM, quality & regulatory, chemistry, and computational research (hey, I love learning new things). While diverse, these experiences have all stimulated my problem solving, analytical, and data-informed side and bring a unique perspective to product.

What do you do for fun?

I love being in nature, traveling abroad, trying new foods, coding, reading books, practicing new skills, and writing. I used to run a travel blog with 50k+ sessions. I currently run a food Instagram... which does not have an audience... but it's fun :).

I either get INTP ("The Architect") or ENTP ("The Visionary"), depending on the day. Today, the ENTP description resonates more:

"ENTPs are inspired innovators, motivated to find new solutions to intellectually challenging problems. They are curious and clever, and seek to comprehend the people, systems, and principles that surround them. [Visionaries are] open-minded and unconventional." (from Truity)
